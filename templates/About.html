About Us

Eunoia is a Ethereal word derived from the Greek language. It denotes "beautiful thinking" or “a state of normal mental health”. Eunoia is often used to describe a mind that is well-balanced and exhibits positive, constructive thoughts. In rhetoric, eunoia refers to the goodwill a speaker fosters between themselves and their audience, an essential aspect of persuasive speaking.
